Hi! We're the Girls Who Code of Decorah, Iowa. Over the past few months we have been working on creating a place where people of any age can connect online and do fun activities!


W.A.T.E.R.M.E.L.O.N. is an acronym for Winneshiek County Activities for Teens to Exchange Resources Meant to Engage Learning, Organization, and Nurture the Mind.
